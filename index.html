Main Insight: Barriers to Seamless Care

The primary legislative gap is the **Over-Centralization** of resources and services in psychiatric hospitals, directly violating the spirit of RA 11036. This creates a supply-side failure where care cannot reach the local PHC/Barangay level where it is most needed.

Co-Creation Strategy for Cultural Humility

  • **Harnessing Filipino Values:** Service design must be rooted in concepts like **KAPWA** (shared identity) and **BAYANIHAN** (communal support) to overcome social **STIGMA**.
  • **Financial Leverage:** The recent massive funding increase (PHP 57M to 1B) must be leveraged to **decentralize** resources away from the few centralized facilities.

Chapter 3: Foundational Structural Pillars

Required Structural Components

  • **Standardized Tools:** Implementation of AI-powered self-assessment and screening for dynamic risk profiling at the community level.
  • **Capacity Building:** Rigorous training of community workers using WHO's mhGAP protocols.
  • **Seamless Navigation:** Establishing clear and simple patient referral pathways between digital, community, and specialist tiers of care.

Cognitio+ Countermeasures to Systemic Barriers

Barrier: Cost Prohibitive Care / Financial Constraints

**Countermeasure:** Implement Tiered/Subsidized Pricing and B2G Contracts to cross-subsidize free basic services for low-income groups.

Barrier: Digital Divide / Geographic Isolation (GIDA)

**Countermeasure:** Develop Offline-Enabled "Lite" Applications and leverage SMS/multi-platform support to reach geographically isolated areas.
